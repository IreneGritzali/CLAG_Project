A Few Things About Us

​

Who we are

We are a group of girls based in Greece and we want to help reduce the percentage of stray animals seeking for a home and help minimize this problem that exists in our world. Every animal deserves a home and we are here to ensure this.

​

What our goal is

We are aiming to advise and inform as many people as possible in the matter of the abondonment of dogs, cats and other animals and the general existence of stray animals. Raising awareness for this subject is the only way to find homes for stray pets.
