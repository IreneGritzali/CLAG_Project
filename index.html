Help a Paw

Save a Life

​

There are over 600 million stray animals all over the world that suffer and live in misery (starving, cold, sickness, and abuse).

In Greece there are almost 1,8 million stray dogs and approximately 2 million stray cats.

In 2016 stray dogs in Greece were calculated to be something less that 2 million and cats surpassed that number. Even though many shelters and volunteers have strived to reduce those numbers the problem still exists.

This dreadful situation needs to stop.
